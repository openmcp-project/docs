# Community

Welcome to the OpenControlPlane community! We're building an open platform for managing cloud infrastructure and services, and we'd love for you to join us.

## About Our Community

OpenControlPlane is organized around [Special Interest Groups (SIGs)](./01-sigs.md) that focus on specific areas of the project. Our community repository at [openmcp-project/community](https://github.com/openmcp-project/community) serves as the central hub for all community-related content, including documentation, resources, and contribution guidelines.

## Get Started Today

### Join SIG Extensibility

If you're interested in building providers (Service Providers, Cluster Providers, or Platform Providers), join our **SIG Extensibility** group:

- **📧 Mailing List**: Subscribe to [openMCP-extensibility@lists.neonephos.org](mailto:openMCP-extensibility@lists.neonephos.org) to receive updates and invitations to our community calls
- **📅 Engineering Calls**: Join us bi-weekly on Wednesday at 3PM CET for public engineering discussions
- **📚 Learn More**: Check out the [SIG Extensibility charter](https://github.com/openmcp-project/community/tree/main/sig-extensibility) for details on scope and how to contribute

These calls are a great way to:
- Learn about provider development
- Share your use cases and ideas
- Get help with your implementations
- Collaborate with other contributors

### Other Ways to Get Involved

- **GitHub**: [openmcp-project](https://github.com/openmcp-project) - Browse our repositories, open issues, and join discussions
- **Contribute Code**: Check our [Contributing Guide](https://github.com/openmcp-project/community/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md) to get started
- **Join a SIG**: Participate in other [Special Interest Groups](./01-sigs.md) aligned with your interests
- **Attend Meetings**: See our full [meeting schedule](./02-meetings.md) across all SIGs

## Code of Conduct

We follow the [Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct](https://github.com/openmcp-project/.github/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) to maintain a welcoming and harassment-free environment for everyone.
